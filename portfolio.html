About Me

Hey! My name's Elaine. I am orignially from Maine. Yes, I know it rhymes...

I went to Simmons College in Boston where I lived for 10 years working in the restaurant industry. I worked at amazing restaurants such as Oleana, Sandrine's Bistro and Sibling Rivalry.

I moved to Oakland 5 years ago and now work for a wine importer/distributor where I do office-y things.

I recently spent a year traveling around the world with my husband and visited 20 countries- Thailand, Cambodia, Vietnam, China (HKSAR), Indonesia, Malaysia, Japan, Nepal, Seychelles, South Africa, Botswana, Zimbabwe, Greece, Egypt, Jordan, England, Peru, Chile, Argentina, Mexico.

I have a passion for all things outdoors and am also a certified Yoga instructor and Spin instructor.

My dog's name is 'Maybe', yes, her name is Maybe... and I have a cat we call Smee. They are both very cute and we spoil them.

Currently I'm in Berkeley Coding Bootcamp where I'm learning a ton and really enjoying how to make websites. It sometimes feels impossible, but I'm finding that chipping away at tasks bit by bit is the way to go.

Namaste!
